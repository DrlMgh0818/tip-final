<h3 class="text-center text-secondary">ABET Accreditation</h3>

ABET, a globally respected not-for-profit organization, has recognized T.I.P. for its high educational standards, accrediting 20 of T.I.P.'s engineering and computing programs. This accreditation is a testament to T.I.P.'s quality of curriculum, faculty, facilities, and institutional support, positioning it as a leader in technological education on an international scale.

<h3 class="text-center text-secondary">ISO 9001:2008 Certification</h3>

The T.I.P.'s Quality Management System has earned ISO 9001:2008 Certification, reflecting its consistent commitment to meeting and exceeding customer expectations and regulatory requirements. This certification, awarded by Det Norske Veritas (DNV), covers all academic offerings and is part of T.I.P.'s continual effort for improvement and customer satisfaction.

T.I.P.'s Quality Management System includes notable components like the 5S program and KAIZEN, showcasing its status as a benchmark for quality management among academic institutions.

Through these accreditations and certifications, T.I.P. affirms its position as a leading institution for technological education, committed to excellence, innovation, and the success of its students on both a local and global scale.

<h3 class="text-center text-secondary">PACUCOA: Level III Accredited</h3>

Our institution has been conferred with Level III Accredited Status in Industrial Engineering by PACUCOA (Philippine Association of Colleges and Universities Commission on Accreditation). This esteemed recognition underscores our commitment to academic excellence and underscores our position as a leading institution in the field of Industrial Engineering. 

PACUCOA accreditation serves as a testament to our commitment to providing high-quality education in Industrial Engineering. It assures students, parents, and stakeholders that our program meets or exceeds national standards and prepares graduates for successful careers in the field.

With this recognition, TIP Manila solidifies its position as a premier institution for engineering education in the Philippines.

<h3 class="text-center text-secondary">PACUCOA: Level III Accredited</h3>

TIP Manila has been accredited by the Philippine Technological Council (PTC). This accreditation serves as a recognition of our institution's adherence to rigorous standards of excellence in technological education.

The accreditation process by the PTC involves a thorough evaluation of various aspects of our academic programs, faculty qualifications, facilities, and institutional governance. By achieving this accreditation, TIP Manila demonstrates its commitment to providing high-quality education that meets the demands of the rapidly evolving technological landscape.

PTC accreditation not only validates the quality of education we offer but also enhances the value of our graduates in the job market. Employers can trust that TIP Manila graduates are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and competencies needed to excel in their chosen fields.
